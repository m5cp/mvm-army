🇺🇸 MVM Fitness

Mission-ready fitness tracking built for performance, not convenience.

MVM Fitness is a military-focused fitness app designed for U.S. Army soldiers, leaders, and athletes who need a fast, reliable, offline-capable system to track performance, build training plans, and dominate the Army Fitness Test (AFT).

---

## ⚡ Core Value

> Train anywhere. Track everything. Share results. No signal required.

MVM Fitness is built for real-world environments:
- field training
- ranges
- deployments
- poor or no connectivity zones

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 🧮 AFT Calculator (Primary Feature)
- Instant scoring for Army Fitness Test
- Works 100% offline
- No login required
- Fast, field-ready input
- Designed for:
  - graders
  - instructors
  - test takers

---

### 🏋️ Training System

#### Individual PT
- Personalized fitness plans
- Structured progression

#### Functional Fitness
- High-intensity, performance-based workouts

#### Unit PT Planning
- Build and manage group workouts
- Designed for leaders and NCOs

---

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- Log workouts
- Track consistency
- View performance over time
- Designed for action, not passive viewing

---

### 🗓 Calendar Integration
- Sync workouts to device calendar
- View past, current, and planned sessions
- Supports long-term planning

---

### 🔗 Share System (Viral Design)

Built for real-world sharing inside units.

#### Share Cards Include:
- AFT Scores
- Completed Workouts
- Training Plans
- Progress

#### Capabilities:
- Export as high-quality image
- Share via iMessage / AirDrop / social
- Generate QR codes for quick transfer

> Designed so users want to share their results

---

### 📱 Offline-First Architecture

MVM Fitness is designed to work without internet.

Fully functional offline:
- AFT Calculator
- Workouts & Plans
- Progress tracking
- Share card generation
- QR code creation

No loading delays. 
No dependency on connectivity. 
Works anywhere.
